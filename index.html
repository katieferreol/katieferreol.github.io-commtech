Components

Eco-Friendly.

The foundation of the Memorabilia™ Frames are made of aluminum, a green material for its long lifespan and reusability.

For the lenses, we use borosilicate, a type of glass that allows for a very low coefficient of thermal expansion.

Brain-Computer Interface.

On the left side of the frames, a small data chip is installed that connects your memories to the projection technology.

To mimic recalling of memories, the projected video will be hazy and muffled, preventing users to grow too dependent on them.

Ant-Hacker Software.

Instead of the user being able to playback their own memories every single time, the memories are only accessible if someone is asking it from you and you give it with your consent.
